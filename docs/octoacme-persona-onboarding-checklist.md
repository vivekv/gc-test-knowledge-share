# OctoAcme Persona Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist helps new team members onboard to their role in OctoAcme projects, ensuring they understand their responsibilities, key interactions, and have access to necessary resources.

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md) to understand OctoAcme's principles and lifecycle
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) to understand your role and how you'll collaborate with others
- [ ] Get added to relevant communication channels (Slack, Teams, email lists)
- [ ] Review the current project charter or one-pager
- [ ] Get access to project management tools (GitHub Projects, Jira, etc.)
- [ ] Attend team standup and introduce yourself
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with key collaborators from other roles
- [ ] Review the [Collaboration Matrix](./octoacme-collaboration-matrix.md) to understand cross-role interactions

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### For Developers
- [ ] Get repository access and clone the codebase
- [ ] Set up local development environment
- [ ] Review coding standards and PR conventions
- [ ] Familiarize with CI/CD pipeline and testing requirements
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](./octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) for workflow details
- [ ] Pick a "good first issue" to get familiar with the codebase

### For Product Managers
- [ ] Review product roadmap and current priorities
- [ ] Understand success metrics and how they're measured
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders to understand their needs
- [ ] Review customer feedback channels and data sources
- [ ] Familiarize with [Project Initiation](./octoacme-project-initiation.md) and [Project Planning](./octoacme-project-planning.md)
- [ ] Schedule regular sync with Project Manager

### For Project Managers
- [ ] Review current project plan, timeline, and dependencies
- [ ] Access risk register and understand current risks
- [ ] Review [Risk Management & Communication](./octoacme-risks-and-communication.md)
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and stakeholder communication needs
- [ ] Familiarize with [Release & Deployment](./octoacme-release-and-deployment.md) processes
- [ ] Schedule weekly syncs with Product Manager and team leads

### For UX Designers
- [ ] Access design tools and asset libraries
- [ ] Review design system and component library
- [ ] Understand user research processes and tools
- [ ] Review existing user personas and research findings
- [ ] Schedule design reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- [ ] Understand accessibility standards and requirements

### For Scrum Masters
- [ ] Review team's current sprint cadence and ceremonies
- [ ] Understand team velocity and historical performance
- [ ] Access sprint planning and retrospective tools
- [ ] Review [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](./octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md)
- [ ] Identify current team impediments and blockers
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with all team members

### For DevOps Engineers
- [ ] Get access to cloud infrastructure and deployment systems
- [ ] Review CI/CD pipeline configuration
- [ ] Understand monitoring, logging, and alerting setup
- [ ] Review incident response procedures and on-call rotation
- [ ] Familiarize with [Release & Deployment](./octoacme-release-and-deployment.md) checklist
- [ ] Review security scanning and compliance requirements

### For Business Analysts
- [ ] Access data analysis tools and reporting dashboards
- [ ] Review existing requirements documentation
- [ ] Understand business objectives and key stakeholders
- [ ] Schedule meetings with business stakeholders
- [ ] Review process flows and business rules
- [ ] Familiarize with [Project Initiation](./octoacme-project-initiation.md) for requirements gathering

## First Week Goals

- [ ] Complete all general and role-specific onboarding items
- [ ] Attend all recurring team meetings to understand rhythm
- [ ] Shadow experienced team members in your role
- [ ] Make at least one small contribution (review, comment, or task)
- [ ] Schedule feedback session with your manager or team lead

## 30-Day Checkpoint

- [ ] Independently complete tasks appropriate for your role
- [ ] Demonstrate understanding of team processes and your responsibilities
- [ ] Build working relationships with key collaborators
- [ ] Provide feedback on the onboarding process for continuous improvement
